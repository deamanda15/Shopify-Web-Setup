# Create and Use Google My Business (Google Business Profile)

This guide explains how to set up, verify, and optimize your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) to drive local search traffic, gain customer reviews, and boost search engine visibility for your brand.

## Steps to Create and Optimize Your Profile:

### 1. Register Your Business on Google
- Go to [google.com/business](https://google.com/business) and sign in with your business Google account.
- Click **Add business** > **Add single business**.
- Enter your exact business name (e.g., *Dafanya Scarf*) and select the appropriate category (e.g., *Boutique*, *Clothing Store*, or *Fashion Accessories Store*).

### 2. Specify Your Location and Service Area
- Choose whether you have a physical store customers can visit:
  *   **If Yes**: Enter your exact shop address (e.g., *Jl. Salam 2 No.15, RT.7/RW.6, Sukabumi Utara, Kec. Kb. Jeruk, Jakarta Barat*). Make sure to place the map pin accurately.
  *   **If No (Online only / Service area)**: Specify your delivery regions (e.g., Indonesia, Jakarta, etc.).
- Add your contact phone number and your website URL (`https://dafanyascarf.id`).

### 3. Verify Your Business Profile
Google requires verification to prevent unauthorized listings. Choose one of the available methods:
- **Phone or SMS Verification**: Enter the code sent to your phone.
- **Email Verification**: Receive a verification link at your domain email.
- **Video Recording**: Record a continuous video showing your business location, equipment, and proof of management (e.g., unlocking the facility or showing official registration documents).
- **Postcard**: Google will mail a physical postcard containing a verification code to your address (usually takes 1-2 weeks). Once received, enter the code in your Google Business Profile dashboard.

### 4. Optimize Profile Details for Local SEO
To improve visibility in Google Maps and local search results:
- **Add Business Hours**: Keep your operating hours updated (e.g., 09.00 - 17.00 WIB).
- **Write a Detailed Description**: Add a compelling summary of your brand (focusing on keywords like "hijab premium", "voal scarf", "boutique hijab Jakarta").
- **Upload High-Quality Photos**: Frequently upload photos of your products, packaging, and store interior to build credibility.
- **Add Products Catalogue**: Use the product editor inside the profile to display featured collections directly on Google search results, linking them back to your Shopify store pages.

### 5. Manage Customer Reviews and Engagement
- **Request Reviews**: Share your custom review link with satisfied customers to collect 5-star ratings. Reviews are a key ranking factor for local searches.
- **Respond to Reviews**: Always respond to reviews (both positive and negative) to demonstrate active customer service.
- **Post Updates**: Use the "Updates" feature to post weekly promotions, discounts, or new arrivals directly on Google Search/Maps.
