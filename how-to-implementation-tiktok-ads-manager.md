# How to Implement TikTok Ads Manager and TikTok Shop Analytics

This guide describes how to configure TikTok Ads, track ad performance metrics, analyze video creative assets, and monitor TikTok Shop affiliate video sales for your store.

## Steps to Implement and Analyze TikTok Ads:

### 1. Set Up TikTok Ads Manager and Pixel
- Create a TikTok Ads Manager account.
- Install the **TikTok Pixel** on your Shopify store (this can be done easily using the official TikTok channel app in Shopify) to track user activities like Page Views, Add to Cart, and Complete Payment.

### 2. Monitor Core Campaign Performance
- Go to the TikTok Ads Manager Dashboard.
- Track key aggregate metrics over selected time periods (e.g., February 2025) to check your return on investment:
  *   **Cost**: Total ad spend (e.g., 1,236,582 IDR).
  *   **Impressions**: Number of times your ads were displayed (e.g., 685,198 impressions).
  *   **Clicks (destination)**: The number of clicks directing users to your store (e.g., 11,450 clicks).
  *   **Conversions**: Completed purchases or targeted actions (e.g., 3,391 conversions).

![TikTok Ads Dashboard Metrics](assets/images/Screenshot%202025-03-03%20091113.png)

### 3. Analyze Video Material Performance
- Navigate to the **Assets** > **Creative** tab in TikTok Ads Manager to evaluate the performance of individual video creatives.
- Compare creative assets using detailed delivery metrics:
  *   **CPC (Cost Per Click)** & **CPM (Cost Per Mille/1,000 impressions)**: Measure traffic cost efficiency.
  *   **CTR (Click-Through Rate)**: High CTR (e.g. 7.38%) indicates engaging video content.
  *   **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)** & **CVR (Conversion Rate)**: Determine which video generates purchases at the lowest cost.

![TikTok Video Ad Creative Performance](assets/images/Screenshot%202025-03-03%20114755.png)

### 4. Monitor TikTok Shop Seller Center & Affiliate Sales
- If you sell directly on TikTok Shop, open the **TikTok Shop Seller Center** under the **Compass** or **Analytics** tab.
- Track performance for both brand videos (`@dafanya_scarf`) and affiliate creator videos:
  *   **GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)**: Total revenue generated by each video (e.g., up to Rp 5,936,754 for top videos).
  *   **SKU Orders / Items Sold**: The volume of products ordered through shoppable video tags.
  *   **Details Analysis**: Click **Details** to see audience demographics, retention rate, and peak buying times in the video.
