# How to Subscribe to Shopify

After successfully linking your domain to Shopify, the next step is to choose a Shopify subscription plan to make your store live.

## Steps to Subscribe:

### 1. Open Shopify Settings
- Log in to your Shopify Admin dashboard.
- In the bottom left corner of the page, click on the **Settings** (gear icon).

![Shopify Admin Settings](assets/images/how%20to%20subs-pict%201.png)

### 2. Navigate to Billing
- In the Settings sidebar, click on **Billing** (or **Plan** if you are choosing a plan for the first time).

![Shopify Settings Menu](assets/images/how%20to%20subs-pict%202.png)

### 3. View Subscriptions and Choose a Plan
- Under the **Subscriptions** section, you will see your active plan or have the option to choose a plan.
- Shopify offers several plans such as **Basic** (suitable for solo entrepreneurs), **Shopify**, and **Advanced**. 
- Select your preferred plan, choose a billing cycle (yearly subscription offers a discount, e.g., Basic plan at $228.00 yearly), and complete your payment setup by adding a credit card, debit card, or PayPal.
